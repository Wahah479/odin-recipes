Agua de Fresa

Description

This Mexican fruit drink is extremely flavorful and refreshing, especially when fresh strawberries are in season. Fresh fruit waters (aguas de frutas) made with crushed or blended fruit are a common and popular drink all over Mexico.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups strawberries, sliced
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 8 cups cold water, divided
  • 5 cups ice for serving, or as needed
  • 1 lime, cut into 8 wedges
  • 8 fresh mint sprigs
